Overview

I was brought onto the ILTA marketing team in late August of 2018 as a W2 contractor for an estimated nine months. My tasks were to complete a full audit of the company's website infrastructure and assist in implementing auotmated marketing solutions. By December I was offered a full time position as a core front end developer, which I readily accepted. Myself and many other employees had our time with ILTA cut short due to COVID-19 being that ILTA's core products and services revolve around in-person events.

Tech

ILTA's website stack was based on vendor solutions, namely the HigherLogic CMS and the Personify360 CRM. Both were outdated and riddled with bad UX, SEO blockers, inaccessibility, and security flaws.

The team's primary operational goal moving into 2019 was to migrate our data from Personify360 into Salesforce and then utilize Salesforce as both CRM and headless CMS. With SF as a headless CMS we would be able to build any front end with any tech stack needed regardless of department. For example, the marketing team may need Wordpress and a visual builder for an event site while operations needed Angular/Cordova for our mobile applications and both would have been compatible with our data structure.
