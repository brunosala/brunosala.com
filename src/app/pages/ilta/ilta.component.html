Overview

I was brought onto the ILTA marketing team in late August of 2018 as a W2 contractor role for an estimated nine months. My initial tasks were to complete a full audit of the company's website infrastructure, coding practices, and assist in implementing auotmated marketing solutions. By December I was offered a full time position as ILTA's sole front end Developer, which I readily accepted. Myself and many other employees had our time with ILTA cut short due to COVID-19 - ILTA's core products and services revolve around in-person events.

Tech

At ILTA, our website stack was based vendor solutions, namely the HigherLogic CMS and the Personify360 CRM. Both were outdated and riddled with bad UX, SEO inaccessibility, and security flaws.

My primary goal moving into 2019, with the rest of the IT/Operations team, was to migrate our data from Personify360 into Salesforce and then utilize Salesforce as both CRM and headless CMS. With SF as a headless CMS we would be able to build any front end with any tech stack needed regardless of department. For example, the marketing team may need Wordpress and a visual builder for an event site while operations needed Angular/Cordova for our mobile applications - both would have been compatible with out back end.
